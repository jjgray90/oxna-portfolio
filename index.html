About Me

I first came across the idea of software development as a career path after leaving my job of 5 years in the music industry. I'd been searching for what I'd like to do next, and stumbled across a coding taster session with _nology. I went along, laptop in hand, with no idea what to expect. 2 hours later I emerged with a basic whack-a-mole web game and an answer to my question, this is what I wanted to do.

Now very much stuck into _nology's 12-week tech consultantcy bootcamp, I am looking forward to placing into a role where I can contribute as part of a team to exciting projects, as well as learning from those around me to become a more accomplished and experienced developer.

Away from the desk I enjoy travelling, running very far (slowly) and rock climbing (badly).
